# Legal Structure

Social Production's collective assets, funds, and property are held by an international non-profit foundation established solely to steward community-owned resources. The foundation has no shareholders and no profit motive. Its founding documents legally bind it to:

- Hold all assets in trust for the Social Production user community
- Take no action with any asset or fund without verified on-chain community consent
- Maintain full public transparency of all holdings, transactions, and fund movements via the blockchain

## Blockchain as Authority

The blockchain is the authoritative record of all community decisions. The foundation exists only to provide legal standing for collective ownership — it does not govern, it does not decide. The community governs through the platform.

## Fiduciary Duty

The foundation's directors are bound by fiduciary duty to execute only what the blockchain records as community decisions. Any action taken without verified on-chain consent constitutes a breach of duty.

## Assets Held

The foundation may hold:

- Bank accounts and financial assets
- Real property and equipment
- Intellectual property
- Digital assets (cryptocurrency, domain names, etc.)

## Director Appointment

Foundation directors are appointed and removed solely through verified on-chain community votes. Directors have no independent authority.
